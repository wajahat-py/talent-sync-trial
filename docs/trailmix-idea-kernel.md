# TrailMix — Idea Kernel

## Raw idea
Day-hike planning is scattered across AllTrails, weather apps, and group chats. TrailMix: pick a date and a vibe ("easy + waterfall + under 90 min away"), it proposes 3 hikes with drive time, weather window, and a packing list, then drops a shareable plan card into the group chat.

## Why now
Post-AllTrails fatigue — discovery is solved, *deciding and coordinating* is not. Group consensus is the unsolved step.

## Riskiest assumption
That groups will adopt a planning artifact (the plan card) instead of just arguing in the chat. If the card doesn't become the focal point, there's no product.

## Smallest test
No app. A web form → generated plan card image → share sheet. Measure: how many cards get re-shared into a second group chat within 14 days.

## Status
Kernel only — not staffed, not scheduled. Parked until two signals: 50 organic card shares, or a hiking-adjacent creator asks for it.
